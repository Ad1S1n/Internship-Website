About our Internship Program

A 10 day program designed to boost your technical skills and give you an insight into the professional workflow. We help you in developing the skills to stand out in a crowded job market. With CodeCeTra Internship, you'll gain hands-on knowledge about how production level operations for Website development and deployment works.

Learn Technical Skills and Get Industrial Exposure by being a part of the CodeCeTra's ecosystem.

About CodeCeTra: We are an MSME registered EdTech Startup which offers customizable courses to learn coding in addition to a watch-time based payment scheme . Our Platform provides a development IDE, an internet hosting for softwares and version control inbuilt in our platform

We here at CodeCeTra go beyond textbooks and lectures to make you learn what it takes to produce professional results in the real world. You'll practice Resume building , LinkedIn account building, Github Deployment and operations along with other crucial skills for success.

Being a part of CodeCeTra Internship Program keeps you Ahead of the Competition and provides with Real world experience in Software practices. We provide this by having a flexible online learning medium for our students.

Guidelines

  1. CIP is an unpaid internship program designed to help students meet the evolving demands of the tech sector.
  2. Make sure you will fill every detail in the registration form correctly and check it before submitting it .
  3. During the internship program, if found guilty of any malpractices like copying the solutions , he/she will be deemed not eligible for the certificate .
  4. Causing distress in the our community group on LinkedIn may lead to disqualification and banning of the candidate .
  5. If you face any issue in any stage of registering in our program kindly email us on contact@codecetra.in
  6. Solutions of the problem statements should be submitted within the given amount of time or else he/she would be getting a certificate of learning.
  7. All the solutions to the problem statement will be posted on CodeCeTra youtube channel following the deadline
